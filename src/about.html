Manufacturing to Software: Engineering Better Lives

With a passion for chemistry, programming and improving people's lives, I have spent 5 years in the medical device manufacturing industry and most recently the last two years in software development.

Being a Manufacturing Engineer for an organic chemistry manufacturing line taught me the analytical, problem solving and leadership skills applicable in all areas of engineering.

When an opportunity came to switch career paths and have an outsized impact on the future of the world, I jumped at the chance to put my skills to use for the Biden for President campaign.

Currently residing in the deep North woods, I'm looking to continue developing my programming and leadership skills at a remote first/remote friendly organization. Feel free to reach out.
